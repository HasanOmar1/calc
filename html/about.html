About

I have created a website for 4th graders, where they can calculate numbers using the arithmetic operators ( + , - , * , / ) and I made it that when you calculate, you see the calculations as images.

I have also added exams for each operation, which you can take on the calculator page.

Notes

- The exams are meant for 4th graders.

- The numbers range for Addition and Subtraction is 0-1000.

- The numbers range for Multiplication is 0-12.

- The numbers range for Division is 1-144.

- The result of the division calculator and exam are rounded. (15.81 = 16 , 9.34 = 9)
